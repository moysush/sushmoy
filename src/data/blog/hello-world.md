## Hello, Everyone!

Welcome to my **personal space** in this digital world. It has been a while since I've been thinking of launching my own personal blog [site](https://sushmoy.pages.dev/), and here it is, at last. I'm excited to share so much with you throughout my journey as a software engineer!

## Struggle

Initiating this project made me go through some difficult choices, for example, choosing the right template or choosing the right place for hosting the site took me on a roller-coaster ride. It may look all easy on the surface, but when we are starting something for the first time, everything seems complex, and we are puzzled by all the infinite choices.

## Choices

I decided to go with the [astro-paper](https://github.com/satnaing/astro-paper) template as it instantly caught my eye with its beautiful theme and all the features it provides (exactly what I was looking for). For hosting the site, I used Cloudflare's pages to get those clean-looking `pages.dev` domain. I've also added my [site](https://sushmoy.pages.dev/) in the Google search console so that it can easily be found when searched. Lastly, for the favicon, I decided to use an avatar that I created using [dicebear](https://www.dicebear.com/), an open-source platform for avatar creation. I went with the notionist style as its minimalism resonated more with me. Here's the avatar I came up with:
